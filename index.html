About Us

A personal touch in education recruitment

Classroom Support is a leading supplier of Teaching Assistants for both SEN and mainstream schools throughout London, Kent & the South East. We offer recruitment solutions for short-term, long-term and permanent vacancies.

Our dedicated consultants take the time to understand your school's individual culture and needs — so every placement is a genuinely great fit, not just a name on a list.

  • Specialist SEND recruitment expertise
  • Rigorous vetting & DBS safeguarding
  • Dedicated personal consultants
  • Short-term, long-term & permanent placements

Our Mission

Ensuring every child achieves their full potential

At Classroom Support, we are passionate about the children we support. We recognise that the right staff play an integral part in the positive learning experiences for children in all schools — whether SEN, mainstream, or specialist.

It's about giving children a life without limits — opening doors and showing them that anything is possible. There is nothing more rewarding than watching a child with additional needs make connections with the world around them.

Our Mission

Ensuring every child achieves their full potential

At Classroom Support, we are passionate about the children we support. We recognise that the right staff have an integral part to play in the positive learning experiences for children in all schools.

It's about giving children a life without limits — opening doors and showing them that anything is possible. There is nothing more rewarding than watching a child with additional needs make connections with the world around them.

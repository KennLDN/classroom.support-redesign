Our Approach

A recruitment partner that truly knows your school

Classroom Support is a leading supplier of Teaching Assistants for both SEN and mainstream schools throughout London, Kent & the South East. We offer recruitment solutions for short-term, long-term and permanent vacancies.

We focus on providing a personal service at all times — taking the time to get to know you and your individual school, helping us to provide a personally tailored service and ensure that the staff we provide are a perfect fit for your needs.

  • Short-term, long-term & permanent placements
  • No charge until you find someone you wish to hire
  • Competitive temporary-to-permanent options
  • Bespoke staffing solutions for specialist settings

Specialist Settings

We work with all types of schools

Our consultants have deep experience across a wide range of educational settings, from specialist SEN schools to mainstream academies.

🌟

Special Schools

Experienced staff for specialist settings supporting pupils with a wide range of complex needs, including autism, social, emotional and mental health (SEMH), and physical disabilities.

🏢

Pupil Referral Units

Resilient, experienced TAs and teachers who understand the unique challenges of PRU settings and can build strong, trusting relationships with young people who need it most.

🔄

Alternative Provisions

Flexible staffing for alternative provision settings, including online and blended learning environments, with candidates who can adapt quickly and effectively.

🏫

Mainstream SEN Provisions

Highly qualified TAs and teachers to support SEN units and resource bases within mainstream schools, providing specialist support alongside a mainstream education.

Vetting & Safeguarding

Safeguarding is our number one priority

There is nothing Classroom Support takes more seriously than our vetting and safeguarding process. Our processes are fully in line with DfE and REC guidelines — every single time.

✓
Face-to-face interview
✓
Right to work check
✓
Photo ID and address check
✓
Children's Barred List check (List 99)
✓
Enhanced DBS — Update Service registered
✓
Teaching Regulation Agency (TRA) check
✓
Medical check
✓
Minimum two professional references
✓
Relevant qualifications check
✓
Childcare Disqualification Questionnaire

For every supply booking, your dedicated consultant sends a Classroom Support Vetting & Safeguarding document confirming all of the above — simplifying your safeguarding records for supply staff.

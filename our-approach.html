Our Approach

A bespoke service built around your needs

Classroom Support is a leading supplier of Teaching Assistants for SEN and mainstream schools throughout London, Kent & the South East — offering short-term, long-term and permanent vacancies.

Our dedicated consultants work closely with Special Schools, PRUs, Alternative Provisions and SEN provisions within mainstream schools — providing each a bespoke staffing solution.

Our Approach

A bespoke service built around your needs

Classroom Support is a leading supplier of Teaching Assistants for both SEN and mainstream schools throughout London, Kent & the South East. We offer recruitment solutions for short-term, long-term and permanent vacancies.

Our team of dedicated Consultants work closely with a host of Special Schools, Pupil Referral Units (PRUs), Alternative Provisions and SEN provisions within mainstream schools — providing each a bespoke solution for their staffing needs.

  • Personal service tailored to each individual school
  • Specialist knowledge of SEN and SEND recruitment
  • Long-term relationships, not transactional placements
  • Dedicated consultant who gets to know your setting

Our Values

What drives everything we do

At the heart of Classroom Support is a set of values that guide every decision we make.

❤️

Children First

Every decision we make centres on the welfare and development of the children in the schools we serve. This isn't just a policy — it's a genuine belief.

🛡️

Safeguarding Without Compromise

We never cut corners on compliance. Every candidate is personally interviewed and fully vetted — every single time, no exceptions.

🤝

True Partnership

We view our schools and candidates as long-term partners, not transactions. We invest in understanding their needs so our service keeps improving.

✨

Quality Over Quantity

We'd rather make one outstanding placement than ten mediocre ones. Your school's culture and the child's needs always guide us.

💬

Open Communication

We're always accessible, honest and proactive. If there's an issue, we deal with it. If you need something, we're on it — fast.

🌱

Supporting Growth

We don't just place candidates — we support their development. From CV advice to career guidance, we're invested in your long-term success.

Vetting & Safeguarding

Our rigorous safeguarding process

There is nothing Classroom Support takes more seriously than the safety of the children in the schools we serve. Our vetting processes fully align with DfE and REC guidelines.

✓

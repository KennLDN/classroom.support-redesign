The Real Reward

Shape the future of the next generation

Yes, qualifications are important — but with SEN teaching it's about so much more. It's about preparing children who are most in need for their lives, helping them to be successful, get a job and ultimately live independently one day.

There is nothing more rewarding than watching a child with additional needs master a situation and make connections with the world around them. Without a doubt, one of the most inspiring parts of SEN teaching is that you get to learn from your students every day.

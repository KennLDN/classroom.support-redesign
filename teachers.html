For Teachers

Your career. Your terms. Your perfect school.

Classroom Support works closely with Primary, Secondary and SEND schools to provide high-quality teachers who deliver excellent outcomes in the classroom. We focus on fast, reliable staffing solutions and a thorough understanding of each school's curriculum, culture and expectations.

Whether you are an Early Career Teacher, an experienced practitioner, or a subject specialist, we are committed to supporting you at every stage of your teaching journey.

  • Roles across Primary, Secondary and SEND
  • Short-term supply to permanent positions
  • Fast, streamlined compliance process
  • Ongoing support from your dedicated consultant
